Are you at risk for type 2 diabetes?

Diabetes is characterized by high blood sugar levels in the body. If not controlled, it can cause heart attacks, strokes, or kidney damage. Calculating the risk can help prevent possible complications of the disease.

Alert!

Early symptoms are frequent urination and feeling thirsty and hungry.
